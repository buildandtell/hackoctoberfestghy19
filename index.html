What is this?

Welcome to Hackoctoberfest Guwahati, students and professionals of all levels are welcome! This is a month long celebration of OpenSource in the form of a challenge called Hackoctoberfest. You're supposed to make 4 PRs to a GitHub repository anytime between Oct 1 and Oct 31 to complete the challenge.
Don't know what Opensource, PR, Repository, GitHub, mean? perfect! That means you're going to lean a lot! The way this will work for you is simple:

  • Join Hackoctoberfest
  • Ask doubts in the WhatsApp group and get answers
  • Attend events and meetups organized in Guwahati
  • Complete HackoctoberFest
We'll be helping and mentoring you throughout the month to get started in OpenSource. This is all about encouraging meaningful contributions to the open source ecosystem ‚Äî for beginners and veterans alike.

How can I participate?

  • Join the WhatsApp group.
  • Follow the instructions here to join Hackoctoberfest.
  • Ask doubts in the WA group.
  • We'll share registration links for the offline events in the WA group.
  • Participate in AMAs organized in the WA group.
If you feel like the WhatsApp group is not sufficient to help you get started, make sure to join us in the offline events.
